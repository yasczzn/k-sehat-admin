Keluarga Sehat Website is an online vaccine registration platform where users can register for vaccines through an online website from various devices anywhere and anytime. Users can register by logging in or registering an account first and then entering personal data related to vaccination data. In addition, participants can also view information related to vaccination procedures and other information about COVID-19.
